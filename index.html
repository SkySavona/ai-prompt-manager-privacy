Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Dojo Innovations, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") and governs data collection and usage related to our product, the AI Prompt Manager browser extension. By using the AI Prompt Manager extension, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of Your Information

The AI Prompt Manager is designed to prioritize your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • Local Data Only: The AI Prompt Manager stores all prompts and related information locally on your device. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers. We do not collect or process any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Data Retention: Prompts stored locally are automatically deleted after 30 days. This expiration ensures your prompt history is periodically cleared, but you can also manually delete the data by clearing the extension’s storage or uninstalling the extension. If you reset your browser's cache, all stored data will also be deleted.
  • Optional Usage Data: If you choose to provide feedback, report issues, or request support, we may collect your email address (if provided voluntarily) and details about the issue you share.

Use of Your Information

If any personal or feedback data is voluntarily submitted to us, it will only be used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your queries or resolve issues.
  • To improve the AI Prompt Manager extension functionality.
  • To notify you of critical updates or changes to the extension.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. However, we may share limited data under the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal processes.
  • To protect and defend the rights or property of Dojo Innovations, LLC.
  • To prevent or address fraud, abuse, or security issues affecting our product.

Tracking User Behavior

The AI Prompt Manager does not track user behavior, collect analytics, or use cookies. All features and interactions occur locally within the browser.

Automatically Collected Information

The extension may log certain actions locally on your device for functionality purposes, such as saving prompt history or maintaining session data. This information is not transmitted outside your device.

Use of Cookies

The AI Prompt Manager does not use cookies. However, your browser may still use cookies for general browsing outside the extension.

Links to Third-Party Sites and Services

The AI Prompt Manager may include links to third-party resources or services, such as Stripe, to facilitate additional features like supporting the development of the extension through donations or payments. These links redirect you to external websites or services.

Stripe Payment Processing: Any payments or financial transactions initiated through the extension are securely processed by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or process financial or payment information. Users are encouraged to review Stripe’s privacy policy for more information on how they handle your data: Stripe Privacy Policy.

Dojo Innovations, LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before proceeding.

Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any information submitted to us voluntarily (e.g., via feedback or support forms). However, as no internet transmission is completely secure:

  • Any data shared with us is at your own risk.
  • You acknowledge inherent limitations to internet security beyond our control.

Your Rights

As a user, you have full control over your local data managed by the AI Prompt Manager:

  • Delete Your Data: You can clear your prompt history or uninstall the extension to remove all stored data.
  • Feedback Opt-Out: If you choose not to provide feedback or contact support, we collect no data at all.

Children Under Thirteen

The AI Prompt Manager does not target or knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the extension and provided personal data, contact us immediately to ensure compliance and deletion.
